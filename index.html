Welcome to the Weather Bulletin App

A weather bulletin is a report that provides information about current or forecasted weather conditions.It can include information such as temperature, precipitation, wind speed and direction, and weather warnings or alerts. Weather bulletins are typically issued by meteorological organizations and are used by the general public, as well as by industries that are affected by weather conditions, such as agriculture, aviation, and shipping. It's important to check weather bulletin regularly especially if you are planning to travel, work or engage in outdoor activities. It can help to prepare for potential weather hazards and make informed decisions about your plans.

This Weather App can used on laptops and desktops as well as public displays.

This App has a link to the data of 45000+ cities without any delay.

This App shows: Name of City, Country Associated, Exact Local time, Temperatures in (Celsius, Farenheit and Kelvin), Air Pressure, Humidity, Sunrise and Sunset Timings, Cloud Percentage, Wind Speed. Weather Status
